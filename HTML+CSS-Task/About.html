About Tech Hub

Fuel your passion for programming at the Tech Hub—a dynamic platform designed for skill improvement and knowledge enhancement. Our initiative, located at the heart of innovation, offers hands-on workshops, mentorship programs, and collaborative projects.

Whether you’re a seasoned developer or new to the tech scene, join us in building a supportive environment for all to thrive and contribute to the growth of a skilled and empowered generation of tech professionals.

Our Impact

12+ technical sessions led by industry mentors

100+ community members

Internships and freelance jobs landed

Growing a self-sustaining tech ecosystem in Mosul

Vision & Goals

We aim to empower a generation of tech professionals through collaboration, sharing, and continuous learning. With support from QAF Lab and IRADA Network, Tech Hub is bridging gaps in tech education.
